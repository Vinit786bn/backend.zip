How Carbon Wallet Works

A seamless ecosystem connecting sustainable land practices with corporate climate goals.

1

Landowners Register

Farmers and FPOs register their land and upload 7/12 ownership documents. Our system captures coordinates and boundaries.

2

AI & Admin Verification

Ollama AI parses documents while satellite scans verify biomass and detect risks. Admins give the final approval to mint credits.

3

Live Trading

Minted credits hit the live order book. Industries buy credits via Razorpay to offset emissions, secured on the blockchain.

Platform Features

dMRV Satellite Scans

Automated digital Measurement, Reporting, and Verification using historical NDVI, biomass estimation, and flood risk analysis.

Blockchain Ledger

Every minted credit and trade is cryptographically hashed, preventing double-counting and ensuring transparent audit trails.

Advanced Order Book

Support for live market orders and automated limit orders. Set a target price and the system matches you when supply hits your bid.

Local AI Document Parsing

Integrated with Nemotron-3 to instantly read uploaded 7/12 registry documents and verify ownership details without human error.
